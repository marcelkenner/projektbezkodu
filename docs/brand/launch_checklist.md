# Launch Checklist

1. **Design review** – Confirm tokens, components, templates aligned with brand spec.
2. **Stakeholder sign-off** – Marcel (sponsor), Magda (design), Piotr (frontend), Agnieszka (content).
3. **Accessibility + performance** – Complete audits outlined in `docs/brand/accessibility_testing.md`.
4. **Preview build** – Deploy Next.js preview (Vercel or AWS) and attach link in tracking board.
5. **Documentation update** – Refresh `docs/brand_design_system_plan.md` and changelog.
6. **Announcement** – Slack/email summary with highlights, upgrade steps, and next sprint focus.
7. **Monitoring** – Enable analytics dashboards and error reporting post-launch.
